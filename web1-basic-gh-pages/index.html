Banana Bread Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cups shortening
  • 6 ripe bananas
  • 4 well beaten eggs
  • 2 & 1/2 cups sifted cake flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup walnuts or macadamia nuts

Method:

Cream together sugar and shortening. Add mashed bananas. Blend wet and dry ingredients together. Do not over mix. Add nuts.

Bake at 350ยบ for 45-50 minutes.
